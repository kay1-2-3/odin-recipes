Pizza Recipe

This is an image of the final result of the recipe

The pizza recipe includes many different ingredients such as cheese, tomato sauce, and whatever else. Good Luck!

Ingredients

  • Cheese (Mozarella & Parmeshan)
  • Wheat Flour
  • High Heat Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix wheat flour
  2. Add oil
  3. Add tomato sauce
  4. Add cheese
  5. Add salt, black pepper, and cumin
  6. Add another layer or cheese on top
