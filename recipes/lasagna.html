Lasagna Recipe

This is an image of the final result of the recipe

The recipe includes many different ingredients such as cheese, tomato sauce, onions, beef, and whatever else. I don't make lasagna; good luck!

Ingredients

  • Cheese (Mozarella & Parmeshan)
  • Wheat Flour
  • High Heat Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onions
  • Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cumin

Steps

  1. Mix wheat flour
  2. Add oil
  3. Add cheese
  4. Add salt, black pepper, and cumin
  5. Add garlic, butter, tomatoes, beef, onions, sauce
  6. Add another layer or cheese on top
